About

UWL Nepalese Society was founded in 2022 by Rabin Basnet and a group of Nepalese Students at UWL. The Society's prior agenda is to be the home to Nepalese students and provide assistance in terms of career opportunities, mental health, and everyday necessities. Since then, the society has been actively engaged in various activities to promote the beauty of the Nepalese cultures, traditions, and beliefs as well as provide assistance regarding mental health and opportunities to the students at the University of West London.
